Phoenix Australia Survey Analysis Dashboard

AI-powered automated pipeline for analyzing training evaluation surveys using the Kirkpatrick Four-Level Model. Built for Phoenix Australia, a mental health non-profit affiliated with the University of Melbourne, supporting 190+ online courses.

Multi-tier fallback architecture: Cache → Gemini AI → SBERT → Semantic similarity
Self-improving system: stores all classifications in SQLite, retrains SBERT from accumulated data
Human-in-the-loop learning: user corrections persist and improve future classifications
Question hashing for deduplication ensures instant lookup of previously seen questions
Statistical pre/post comparison with t-tests, Cohen's d effect sizes, and confidence intervals
Professional LaTeX PDF reports with sentiment analysis and theme extraction

About

Data Scientist with a Master's degree from University of Melbourne and 2 years of software engineering experience at Tata Consultancy Services.

I'm fascinated by the intersection of AI and real-world impact—building systems that don't just crunch numbers, but genuinely help people make better decisions. Whether it's analyzing 100,000+ social media posts to uncover sentiment patterns or automating mental health training evaluations, I thrive on turning messy, complex data into clear, actionable insights.

My approach combines rigorous engineering with curiosity-driven exploration. I believe the best solutions come from deeply understanding both the data and the humans who will use it. Currently exploring AI agents and LLM applications— the frontier where automation meets intelligence.
